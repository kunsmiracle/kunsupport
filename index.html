☁ I.
Pre-Release Preparation things to finish before release
QQ Music — 钱锟 Artist Page ↗
01 YouNi Points (由你积分)
Search "腾讯音乐榜" in QQ Music→ Open the points task page (top-right)

Complete all tasks one by one. Once finished, switch to your next account and repeat the process.

Tencent Music Chart is one of the most important charts for supporting the release.
Open Tencent Music Chart ↗
02 Song Promotion Power (推歌电量)
Search "音乐推" in QQ Music→ 乐迷活动→ 星梦发电站→ Search 钱锟 (KUN)→ Tap 发电

After completing the tasks, switch to your next account and repeat.

Tasks marked with a timer will refresh 4 hours after completion.
Open Song Promotion Power ↗
03 Coin Center (金币中心)

Earn coins that can be used to purchase the song.
1 song purchase = 2 RMB ≈ 14,000 coins

My Page→ 领现金

Complete ads and available tasks to earn coins.

Apple users may have difficulty making purchases, so please try to prepare at least 42,000 coins (14,000 × 3) in advance.
04 Creating Multiple Accounts

One phone number can register:

  • 2 WeChat accounts
  • 5 QQ accounts
